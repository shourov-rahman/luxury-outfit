### Social Proof (Real Customer Results)
**Goal:** Build trust and prove the product works for women just like the visitor. Critical for cold & warm traffic.

- 6-8 real customer photos (Bangladeshi women, traditional dress context)
- Short testimonial snippets with first name
- Star ratings visible
- Emphasis on comfort, fitting, and confidence boost

### Size & Fit Confidence Builder
**Goal:** Eliminate the #1 objection (size accuracy) and reduce purchase anxiety.

- Clear size chart (waist measurements in inches)
- Free exchange policy highlighted prominently
- Reassurance copy

### Use Case Scenarios (Context Visualization)
**Goal:** Help the customer visualize herself using the product in her real life situations.

- 3 scenario cards with images:

  2. Family gathering/Wedding
  3. Daily comfort at home
- Each with micro-benefit tied to that context
